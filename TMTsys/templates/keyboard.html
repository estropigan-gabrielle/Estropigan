A KEYBOARD is one of the most used computer input devices. A keyboard, like an electric typewriter, is made up of buttons that are used to create letters, numbers, and symbols, as well as execute other functions. Most keyboards today are similar, however one or more of the elements indicated before may be absent (e.g., the keypad). The structure and design of keyboards is where they start to diverge the most. Membrane keys are used on some keyboards, whereas mechanical keys are used on others. Some keyboards, like the Saitek keyboard depicted above, have a normal shape, but others are split along the middle and have an ergonomic design.
